- **[Lean Startup](https://www.amazon.com/Lean-Startup-Entrepreneurs-Continuous-Innovation-ebook/dp/B004J4XGN6/ref=sr_1_3?ie=UTF8&qid=1541132378&sr=8-3&keywords=lean+startup)** by Eric Ries
