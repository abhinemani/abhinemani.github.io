Many current or prospective public servants — government employees, civic leaders, NGOs, tech startups, etc — are seeking training in how to get the job done in increasingly digital work environments, but lack the necessary resources. The Academy will include practical, collaborative trainings programs on the essential skills and tools for modern governance, based off of my University of Chicago Civic Technology course I built and taught.
